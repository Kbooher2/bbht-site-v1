Eligibility shouldn't be guesswork.

Bad estimates write off real revenue. Manual verification eats UR coordinators alive. And payer quirks change weekly — but spreadsheets don't.

Pain · Today

Phone calls, payer portals, screenshots into PDFs. A single admission can burn 30+ minutes before a number is in front of intake.

Pain · Today

One workflow. Verification, math, and history in a single view.
