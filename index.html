The Platform

One workflow. Three problems solved.

Built specifically for behavioral health — DTX, IOP, PHP, residential — where eligibility complexity and financial exposure are highest.

⚡

Real-Time Eligibility (AdmitIQ)

Run eligibility verification in seconds while the patient is still on the phone. Multi-payer support, in-network and out-of-network logic built in.

💰

Patient Financial Responsibility

Auto-calculate deductible, OOP, co-insurance, and expected patient responsibility per program — DTX, IOP, PHP — before admission, not after.

📊

Client-Customized Analytics

Embedded QuickSight dashboards tailored to each client's KPIs. Census, denials, AR aging, payer mix — your data, your view.

🔔

Payer Alerts, In Context

Coverage terminations, deductible resets, payer outages — surfaced where your team is working, not buried in an inbox.

🗂️

History & Audit Trail

Every verification logged, searchable, re-runnable. Reconcile months later without leaving the screen.

🔌

EHR-Aware Imports

Pull census and admission data from your existing EHR. Reduce double-entry, increase verification coverage, surface gaps automatically.
